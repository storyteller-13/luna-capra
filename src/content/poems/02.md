how many seconds of longing can a soul 
hold in its aether before losing its spirit
how many minutes of hope can an angel 
hold in its wings before losing the direction
how many hours of daydream can a human 
hold in a vision before losing their anima
how many days of antecipation can a woman 
hold in her memory before losing her allure
how many months of vigilance can a man 
hold in his stance before losing his strength
how many years of silence can a pure heart
hold still before burying its virtuous song

the answer, my friend, is still blowin' in the wind.
