how many seconds of longing can a soul 
hold in its aether before it loses its spirit
how many minutes of hope can an angel 
hold in its wings before it loses its direction
how many hours of daydream can a human 
hold in their vision before they lose their anima
how many days of expectation can a woman 
hold in her memory before she loses her allure
how many months of vigilance can a man 
hold in his posture before he loses his strength
how many years of silence can a pure heart
hold before it buries its most virtuous song

the answer, my friend, is still blowin' in the wind.
