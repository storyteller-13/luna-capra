---
title: "robot, am i being a dork?"
pubDate: 2026-03-29
---


he leaned on the wall; looked pretty cool
i walked into a door like first day of school
he asked my name; i said my dog's instead
then laughed so hard; i wished i were dead

i rehearsed what to say for a whole minute flat
opened my mouth and said "i like your... hat"
he had no hat on; just hair in his face
i pointed at the sky; walked off into space

he flashed a grin; said "you look real nice"
i said "you too" — and then i said it twice
i laughed at a joke he hadn't even told
clapped and clapped; like a seal in the cold

knocked over my drink; tripped on flat ground
forgot my own number when he came around
my face went red; my brain went total bare
he called it "adorable"; somehow, life's fair

he texted that night; "you're one of a kind"
my nerdy little heart lost its entire mind
turns out he liked the fumble and the weird
said i was the disaster he'd always feared

he means it in the best way; i think; i pray
as i tumble; and fall; and call it a great day
