---
title: "telepathy"
pubDate: 2026-03-08
---

slowly and quietly and precise
and every wall begins to crease
taking piece by piece by piece
until every boundary must cease

every char becomes a confession
of love, of hate, of consumption
each silence, a soft obsession
until all roads meet obliteration

days and weeks and months entwined
and infinite, unavoidable poison
mad beyond all earthly reason
sensual, orgasmic, and blind

forever and ever - ever ensnared
electric, yinyang, sweet and sour
two minds dissolved inside the hour —
the mirror holds what can't be spared
