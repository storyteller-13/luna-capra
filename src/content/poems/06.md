---
title: "what do you want from me?"
pubDate: 2026-04-12
---


do you want the moon i keep in a purple jar
or the map that i drew to my wishing star?
do you want my laugh; the one that spills
or the wildflowers spilling down my hills?
do you want my sword; both rough and bright
or my sleepy thoughts in the middle of night?
my sky-sipped dreams? my earthly-sown song?
or just someone who'll stay a little long?

now, tell me truly; tell me sweet
the way i dance down every street?
or the way i hum without a reason,
changing tune with in every season?

whatever it is; just say it plain!
like a rainbow after an endless rain,
i'll pour it out; let it flow and be
tell me, what do you want from me?
