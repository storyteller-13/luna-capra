---
title: "when spring arrived"
pubDate: 2026-03-23
---

when the winter started
i was not ready nor did i know
it would be the longest season
and the hardest and the coldest
and nobody cared to tell me

when the winter started
my voice became even quieter
no matter how much i screamed
and nobody could be reached

when the winter started
i was naked on the floor
and the floor was gone
and nobody came to help me

when the winter started
i had already lost everything
things i didn't even know i had
and nobody would pay for the damage

when the winter was over
i still didn't know how why who
i still held a muted voice
i still waited for him

but the spring brought the news
and the flowers and the fruits
and the soul and the promises
and the reasons and the hope
and the friends and the home
and the colors and flavors

and then i finally remembered
the winter could never have killed me
because i am and always will be the sun
