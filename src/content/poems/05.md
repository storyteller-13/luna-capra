we are threes in the same forest
never tangled; never claimed
no promises sworn between us
no initials carved or named

not passing; not possessing
years of quiet standing near
season after season rooted
faithful on each turning year

your branches seek their own light
mine bend toward a different sky
yet the same wind stirs us all
the same clouds drifting by

we do not reach to hold each other
only to remain true through storm
and through the unnamed names 
through silence and through war

we have always stood like this: 
not entwined but side by side
trees in one; one long road
with nothing left to be or hide
